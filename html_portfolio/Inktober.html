The challenge Inktober

Its story

The Inktober challenge is a challenge created by Jake Parker and its goal is to develop your imagination while drawing every day. This challenge takes place over 1 month and for the themes, we can either choose the themes given officially, or choose those created by another artist or create them ourselves. For this challenge I took the themes of the Inktober of an artist named Yanska on Instagram. So the theme is monsters.
The challenge was fun, instructive and exhausting at the same time. Not having much time sometimes, I went to bed very late at night to be able to finish my drawings so due to lack of time I could only do 9 drawings. But this does not prevent me from learning a lot of things with new styles, lights and shadows but also from developing my imagination and discovering a new aspect of drawing.
During the challenge, I undertook to draw on the following themes: Leshy, Familiar, Bat, Drake, Ghost, Vampire, Babayaga, Goblin.
