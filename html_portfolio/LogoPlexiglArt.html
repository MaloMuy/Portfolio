The Logo of Plexigl'art

Its history

I have a friend who had an idea. It wad create Led lamps by engraving an image on a plexiglass plate and creating a base to hold this plate and also to be able to insert leds in order to illuminate the engraved drawing. He plans to sell these leds.
To facilitate communication, he needed a logo, designs and a name. By searching in our head, we found a name: Plexigl'Art.
After finding a name, we looked for the logo. We needed a simple and effective logo that would fit into the base of the lamp and reflect the project. So we did many tries and came up with a nozzle that draws the main letters of the logo.
