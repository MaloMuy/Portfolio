The Logo of the Green Team

Its history

In moral and civic education in high school, I had to create a poster for a political side in groups of 4. The political side we had chosen to create is an ecology politic that we named : Green Team.
Let's now introduce the research of the design. First of all, as the side is on the theme of ecology, I immediately thought of a poster with green colors. Then for the logo, as the leaf is often used to represent something ecological I wanted to use it. So we see the planet Earth with leaves spinning around. The brown color instead of black for the planet Earth allows to bring back a link with the earth.
For the second page, I chose to put the logo in big size to put it in front with the texts which surrounds it a little to be able to have this link between the planet and what we say. I also made small drawings which are matching with the text.
